organization plan

1) Budgeting: Prepare the required lists, make a budget that is expected to arise.

2) Register location: Select the location where the ad will take place. For example, large parks, university yards, exhibition centers, stadiums, etc.

3) Prepare material elements: Arrange and plan the products and materials needed for installation, construction, and transportation. Ensure construction progress, complete on time fairs and exhibitions.

4) Prepare the human factor: Arrange to mobilize personnel according to the right capacity, ensure the good operation of the planned programs.

5) Organize and build booths: Make a map of the number of booths, design event booths, set up booths, design interiors, search for businesses to rent booths, or rent booths. Overall staging.

6) Communication plan: Develop a plan to promote to businesses participating in exhibitions and fairs. Build media projects that attract participants.

Regulations on goods and services at trade fairs and exhibitions

* For events held in Vietnam, goods and services participating in trade fairs and exhibitions must comply with the following conditions:

- Products are not banned from business, restricted from trading, or have not been allowed to be circulated as prescribed by law;

- Products are not banned from import according to the provisions of law. (for goods and services provided abroad)

- The product is a fake, infringing on intellectual property rights. Except for the case of display and introduction for the purpose of comparing with the real goods.
